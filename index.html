FAQ

Common Questions

How much does a small move cost?
Every move is unique, so costs vary based on distance, volume, and services needed. The good news? Small moves are our specialty and often more affordable than you'd expect. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your specific situation.
Do you move pianos?
Absolutely! Piano moving is one of our specialties. We have the proper equipment, padding, and trained professionals to safely move uprights, baby grands, and full concert grands. Your instrument is in expert hands.
What areas do you serve?
We serve all of Chicagoland ‚Äî from the city to the far suburbs. Based in Villa Park, IL, we cover Chicago, DuPage County, Cook County, Will County, Kane County, Lake County, and beyond. If you're in the greater Chicago area, we can help.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes! We are fully licensed with the U.S. Department of Transportation (USDOT #570493) and carry comprehensive insurance. Your belongings are protected from pickup to delivery.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance, though we can often accommodate last-minute moves. Weekends and month-end dates fill up fastest, so the earlier you book, the better your chances of getting your preferred date and time.
Do you offer packing services?
Yes! We offer full packing services using professional-grade materials. Whether you need us to pack everything or just the fragile items, we've got you covered. We also supply boxes and packing materials if you prefer to pack yourself.
